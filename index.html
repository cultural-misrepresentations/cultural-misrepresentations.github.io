Cultural Representation of AI-generated Stories

Examining Bias and Cultural Accuracy in Large Language Models

About This Project

This project evaluates cultural misrepresentations in stories generated by large language models, focusing on how Indian cultural identities are portrayed across regions, traditions, and social contexts.
